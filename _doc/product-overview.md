# John Carefulness — Product Overview

## What It Is

John Carefulness (johncarefulness.store) is a curated affiliate e-commerce storefront. It presents a hand-selected catalog of products from external retail partners. Visitors browse, discover items they want, and click through to the partner's external store to complete their purchase. Revenue is generated through affiliate commissions earned on those click-throughs and resulting purchases.

## Users

- **Shopper** — Online consumers seeking trustworthy, hand-curated product recommendations. Values editorial judgment over algorithmic feeds.
- **Curator / Operator** — John, the store owner, who selects products, writes editorial descriptions, manages listings, and maximises affiliate earnings.

## Positioning

A deliberate, trust-first product discovery platform. The brand name "Carefulness" signals intentional curation — quality over quantity, considered over impulsive. Every listed product is hand-selected; that editorial voice is the competitive moat against generic affiliate aggregators.

## Brand & Tone

Editorial, trustworthy, calm. Not a bargain-hunter's deal aggregator — a considered recommendation platform. Visual language is minimal and premium.

## Core Workflow

1. Visitor lands on the store homepage
2. Browses curated product catalog (by category or search)
3. Opens a product detail page (editorial description, images, curator's reasoning)
4. Clicks the affiliate "Buy at [Partner Store]" link
5. Completes purchase at the external partner's site → commission earned

## Revenue Model

Affiliate commissions: a percentage of each sale completed at a partner store after a click-through from johncarefulness.store.

## Scope (MVP)

- Curated product catalog with category filtering and search
- Product detail pages with editorial descriptions and affiliate links to external stores
- Clean, conversion-optimised browse experience designed to drive affiliate click-throughs
- Curator tooling for managing product listings and affiliate links
